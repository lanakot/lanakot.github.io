About me

Over 25 years experience as a pianist

I have a Master's Degree in Piano Performance from Kiev State Conservatory. I have performed concerts as a soloist and accompanist throughout Europe and North America. Currently, I am the Music Director at St. John's United Church in Oakville, ON, where I am an organist and choir conductor.

I have a private studio where I conduct piano and theory lessons at all skill levels. With over twenty-five years of experience, I know how to tailor my approach to satisfy your childs penchant for learning.

Why Learn to Play?

It's a fun activity that everyone can enjoy.
It can relieve stress! It can relieve stress and improve mental faculties, including concentration, memory, and creative thinking.
Few things will impress your friends more than a flawless piano performance. Give them a performance to remember!
Studying music, especially at a young age, is known to be associated with higher grades in school.
